About

Designing spaces
that tell stories

I'm Divya Bhatt — an architect, interior designer, and educator with over 8 years of experience creating environments that resonate with the people who inhabit them. As the Founder of EVaayu Design Studio, I lead projects ranging from intimate residences to large-scale commercial transformations across Delhi NCR.

My approach weaves together traditional craftsmanship and contemporary sensibility — each design decision grounded in function, materiality, and the lived experience of space. From concept to execution, I bring precision, creativity, and a deep understanding of the built environment.

Beyond practice, I teach architecture and share design knowledge through digital content, reaching millions of homeowners and design enthusiasts across India.
